About

I've worn many hats over the years and gained valuable experience. Most recently as a project manager turn developer, I've had the incredible opportunity to solve linguistic follies for clients. Copy and line editing texts form a foreign language into English really hones my pattern recognition and taight me to simplify, condense, and really think about what matters. Now, as a developer, I'm excited to bring these skills with me and continue to grow as a developer as I recognize patterns, simplify and refactor code and continue to ask, "is this feature really necessary?"

I am a seasoned learner with a dogged determination that has molded me into a versatile, ‘Jane-of-all-trades’ developer with a passion for diversity & inclusion and penchant for clean and simple design.

When I'm not coding and learning new tech stacks, I'm off learning new languages and reading books with my lovely cats. Shoot me an email and ask me how many cats, or for any and all collaborations! I would love to hear from you!

Neatify

Welcome to Neatify.
Neatify is my first fullstack MEHN (MongoDb, Express.js, Handlebars, Node.js) application, using Bootstrap components for frontend design. This application is built on the premise that good writing, like good coding, is a skill that must be practiced. So I created a web app where people can come and practice editing and share their solutions with peers. I continue returning to this project because it is the one I struggled the most yet learned the most from. I'm currently working on user authentication right now so I can have users login and create his/her userpage. Please join me in my journy in completing Neatify!
